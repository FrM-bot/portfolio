## Design Context

### Users
Recruiters and developers evaluating Franco Maciel as a potential hire. Recruiters scan quickly for signals of capability and professionalism; developers dig deeper and evaluate execution quality. Both arrive with some skepticism — they've seen hundreds of dev portfolios. The job-to-be-done: convince them he's worth a conversation, and leave a distinct impression after they close the tab.

### Brand Personality
Elegant, modern, serious. Not warm or playful — composed and deliberate. The impression should be: this person is precise, confident, and builds things with intention. Reference: operaneon.com — editorial typography, generous whitespace, asymmetric compositions, strong hierarchy, purposeful animations and micro-interactions. Anti-reference: nothing warm/amber, nothing craft/handmade, nothing that feels like a template or a bootcamp portfolio.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Light mode default (clean white), dark mode fully designed — not just a color inversion
- **Color**: Cold-neutral palette. Neutrals tinted slightly cool toward the accent hue.
  - Light surface: `oklch(0.98 0.003 255)` — near-pure white with imperceptible cool cast
  - Dark surface: `oklch(0.09 0.006 258)` — near-black with slight blue cast (more editorial than pure black)
  - Accent (light): `oklch(0.40 0.14 258)` — deep indigo, used sparingly
  - Accent (dark): `oklch(0.65 0.14 258)` — same hue, lighter for contrast
  - OKLCH throughout. No HSL. No warm tints.
- **Typography**: Precision grotesque pair.
  - Display/Headings: **Jost** (variable, Google Fonts) — geometric, Futura-lineage, authoritative at bold weights. Not on the reflex ban list.
  - Body: **Manrope** (variable, Google Fonts) — geometric humanist, clean, excellent at small sizes.
  - Large heading weights create editorial impact. Body is comfortable and refined.
- **Layout**: Generous whitespace (operaneon signature). Strong typographic hierarchy. Asymmetric compositions where they add energy. No identical card grids. No everything-in-cards approach.
- **Motion**: Staggered entrance animations on page load. Scroll-driven reveals. Smooth light/dark transitions. Hover micro-interactions on links and interactive surfaces. Exponential easing only — no bounce, no elastic.

### Design Principles
1. **Typography is the architecture.** Hierarchy, weight contrast, and spacing do the design work. Not gradients, not shadows, not decorative borders.
2. **Cold precision.** Every surface tints cool toward the accent hue. No warmth, no roundness, no friendliness. Composed authority.
3. **Motion signals quality.** Animations are purposeful and orchestrated — staggered reveals feel considered, not scattered. One well-choreographed entrance beats ten random micro-interactions.
4. **Accent earns its place.** One color. Used rarely. Maximum impact because of its rarity.
5. **Whitespace is the message.** Generous spacing between sections signals that nothing is crammed in. Quality over density.
